# TRADITIONAL MASTERS POOL WORKOUT: THRESHOLD ENGINE & SPEED RESERVE
**Total Distance: 3,800 Yards / Meters**

---

### 1. Warm-Up (900 Total)
*   **1 x 400 Freestyle** 
    *   *Focus:* Continuous, easy cruise. Maintain a long, clean stroke length.
*   **4 x 75 Stroke Progression** 
    *   *Focus:* 25 Butterfly, 25 Backstroke, 25 Breaststroke. Focus on legal turns and transition mechanics.
*   **4 x 50 Choice Kick** 
    *   *Focus:* Progressive build (1–4) to prime the lower extremities for high-velocity efforts.

---

### 2. Preset: Neuromuscular Speed Activation (600 Total)
**Execute 3 Rounds of:**
*   **2 x 50 Choice Stroke**
    *   *Focus:* Maximum underwater streamline off the wall. Build speed through the 50, finishing at 100m sprint pace.
*   **2 x 50 Freestyle**
    *   *Focus:* Rapid, crisp hand entry and an immediate early vertical forearm catch.
    *   *Coaching Note:* Take an extra 20 seconds of rest between rounds to ensure complete ATP-PC system recovery. This is for neural firing, not metabolic exhaustion.

---

### 3. Main Set: The Cross-Discipline Engine Room (2,000 Total)
*This set alternates between sustained anaerobic threshold blocks (the "Engine") and high-intensity, discipline-specific race pace efforts (the "Speed Reserve").*

#### Block A: The Threshold Base
*   **1 x 500 Freestyle Pull** 
    *   *Focus:* Controlled, steady effort at 90-92% of threshold. This builds foundational aerobic endurance mandatory for marathon swimmers and long-course triathletes alike.

#### Block B: Traditional Swim Meet Race Pace (Repeat 2x)
*   **1 x 200 Specialty Stroke (e.g., 200 Back, 200 Free, or 200 IM)** 
    *   *Focus:* Maintained at your targeted swim meet race pace. Focus on identical stroke counts across each 50 length.
*   **4 x 50 Freestyle Sprint** 
    *   *Focus:* Hold these at your goal 100 Free split velocity. High turn velocity and tight breathing discipline coming off the walls are required.

#### Block C: The Threshold Build
*   **1 x 400 Freestyle Swim** 
    *   *Focus:* Descend by 100s. Start at cruise pace and increase speed every 100m, finishing the final length at absolute threshold velocity.

#### Block D: Lactate Clearance & Speed Reserve (Repeat 2x)
*   **1 x 100 Specialty Stroke (e.g., 100 Free, 100 Back, or 100 Fly)** 
    *   *Focus:* All-out time trial from a push.
*   **4 x 25 Choice Recovery** 
    *   *Focus:* Super easy, active recovery to flush high accumulation of blood lactate from the muscle tissue.

---

### 4. Cool-Down (300 Total)
*   **6 x 50 Choice Recovery** 
    *   *Focus:* Alternating 50 backstroke and 50 easy freestyle. Keep your heart rate below 110 BPM to initiate systemic active recovery protocols [Source 03].
